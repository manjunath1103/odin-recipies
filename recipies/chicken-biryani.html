Chicken-Biryani

Description:

Chicken biryani is a delicious Indian/Pakistani rice dish that's typically reserved for special occasions such as weddings, parties, or holidays such as Ramadan. It has a lengthy preparation, but the work is definitely worth it. For biryani, basmati rice is the ideal variety to use.

Ingredients:

  • Oil
  • Spices
  • Onion
  • Chilli paste
  • Coriander and mint paste
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 2 Tomato
  • Chilli Powder
  • Gharam Masala
  • Basumati Rice
  • Salt
  • Curd
  • Chicken
  • Turmeric Powder

Steps:

  1. Soak the Rice in water
  2. Marinate the chicken with curd with 1/4 spoon turmeric powder and 1 spoon salt
  3. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large skillet.
  4. Add the spices and onion and wait till onion turn golden color
  5. Add Chilli, Coriander, mint and Ginger Garlic Paste
  6. Mix it well
  7. Add tomato and add 1 spoon chilli powder, 1 spoon Gharam masla and mix it will.
  8. Add the marinated chicken and mix it well and cook over very low heat until chicken is tender, 35 to 45 minutes.
  9. Then drain the chicken and add water into it. And mix it well.
  10. Reduce heat to very low, cover with a tight-fitting lid, and steam for 20 minutes without lifting the lid or stirring.
  11. Spoon biryani onto a warm serving dish.
