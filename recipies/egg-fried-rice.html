Egg-Fried-Rice

Description:

This egg fried rice made with simple ingredients is quick and easy.

Ingredients:

  • cup water
  • tablespoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup uncooked instant rice
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup green beans
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten

Steps:

  1. Bring water, soy sauce, and salt to a boil in a medium saucepan. Stir in rice and remove from heat. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a medium skillet or wok over medium heat. Sauté onions and green beans in hot oil for 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Pour in beaten egg and fry for 2 minutes, scrambling egg while it cooks.
  4. Add cooked rice to egg mixture; mix well. Season with pepper.
